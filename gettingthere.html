Venue

The workshop will be hosted in Room 2.15, the School of Computer Science, University of Manchester. The school is located in the Kilburn Building on Oxford Road. There is a map of the campus available here. The Kilburn building is number 39 on the map. The Ibis Hotel is to the west of location 11. Manchester Business School accommodation is in location 29.

By Rail

The IBIS hotel is between Manchester Piccadilly Station and Manchester Oxford Road station. It is less than ten minutes walk from each.

There is a fast efficient service to Manchester Piccadilly Railway Station from many stations in the U.K.. Check the national rail enquiries site for details.

The live departures board for Manchester Piccadilly Railway Station is available here

By Air

Manchester Airport

Manchester Airport is a 15 minute train ride away from Manchester Piccadilly station. Trains depart regularly, about every five minutes, from the airport to the station.

Other Airports

It is also possible to travel by train from other U.K. airports (Heathrow, Gatwick, Manchester, Luton, Stansted). However, in each case the journey is over two hours.
